    Awards/Achievements

    • He was given the 1981 Shogakukan Manga Award for best shōnen or shōjo manga with Dr. Slump, which went on to sell over 35 million copies in Japan alone.
    • His next series, the Dragon Ball, became one of the most successful and popular Manga in the world. It sold 250-300 million copies worldwide.
    • He was presented the ‘Prix Special 40th Anniversary Festival Award’ at France’s ‘Angoulōme International Comics Festival’ in 2013.

    Akira Toriyama is a Japanese manga writer, manga artist, and character designer for video games. He has been a working artist since 1978. In manga, he is better known for creating the science fiction comedy series "Dr. Slump" (1980-1984) and the martial-arts-themed series "Dragon Ball" (1984-1995).
